## 6. Alignment & Coherence Protocol
Before adding anything:

1. **Check alignment:**  
   Does it match T‑series logic?

2. **Check coherence:**  
   Does it contradict Mudpuppy or RTT/3?

3. **Check drift:**  
   Is the language resonance‑first and minimal?

4. **Check placement:**  
   Does it belong in SoftKitty or another module?

AIs should run a **full re‑evaluation** of any new content before merging.
